Aquariums

An aquarium is a vivarium of any size having at least one transparent side in which aquatic plants or animals are kept and displayed. Fishkeepers use aquaria to keep fish, invertebrates, amphibians, aquatic reptiles, such as turtles, and aquatic plants.

About

AquaEscapes is a hub for anyone who is interested in the aquarist hobby. It was created to educate and share knowledge upon aquariums or aquascapes in hopes to support and build the community. With online users from all over the world, it brings together a school of experienced aquarists, to those just starting out. It is also a place for users to post their creative work for anyone to view and like. People may also engage with one another by asking and answering questions or chatting on any related topics. We hope to provide a safe space to those who love and enjoy the hobby as much as we do. Thank you for visiting and please enjoy!
